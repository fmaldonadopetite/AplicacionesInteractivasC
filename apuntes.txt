 ANSI Escape Codes:
 printf(\x1B[38;2;R;G;Bm")  //FG Color
 printf(\x1B[48;2;R;G;Bm")  //BG Color
 printf(\x1B[Y;XH")         //Move cursor to X,Y - Upper left corner is (1,1)
 printf(\x1B[2J")           //Clear Screen
 printf(\x1B[?25l")         //Hide Cursor
 printf(\x1B[?25h")         //Show Cursor
